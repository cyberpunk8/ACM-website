Future-Ready With ACM


  • 1. Learning & Library

    Vast Collection : Access journals, conference papers, magazines, and technical reports.

    Skill Development : Courses, webinars, and tutorials in areas like AI, ML, cybersecurity, and full-stack development.

    Certification & Training : Get professional development certificates to boost your career.

  • 2. Competitions & Events

    ACM ICPC (International Collegiate Programming Contest) : World's most prestigious coding contest for university students.

    ACM Student Research Competition (SRC) : Showcase research projects and compete globally.

    ACM Tech Talks : Live sessions from industry leaders on trending tech topics.

  • 3. Internships & Career Opportunities

    ACM Career & Job Center : Find internships, job postings, and career resources in computing and software engineering.

    ACM Mentorship Programs : Learn from experienced professionals, researchers, and industry leaders.

    Industry Partnerships :ACM collaborates with companies like Google, Microsoft, IBM, and Amazon for career opportunities.

  • 4. Networking With Tech Leaders

    Exclusive Webinars & Panel Discussions : Engage with leaders from Google, Microsoft, IBM, and more.

    Leadership Roles in ACM Chapters : Work alongside industry leaders in student & professional chapters.

    Flagship ACM Conferences : Network with tech visionaries at SIGGRAPH, KDD, ICSE, CHI, and FSE.

 

OUR VISION

"The ACM Student Chapter aims to be a leading community that fosters innovation in computing while educating learners on its significance in the modern world."

OUR MISSISON

Bringing together computing educators, researchers, and professionals to foster collaboration, exchange knowledge, and tackle the challenges of the field."
