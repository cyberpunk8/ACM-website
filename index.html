Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive our initiatives and shape our future

Our Mission

To foster a dynamic environment that promotes technological excellence, collaborative learning, and professional development among computing students at Banasthali Vidyapith.

We aim to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements by providing practical exposure, mentorship, and networking opportunities to our members.

Our Vision

To become a premier student chapter that empowers women in technology and establishes Banasthali Vidyapith as a center of computing innovation and excellence.

We envision our members as future tech leaders who drive positive change in society through ethical application of computing knowledge and skills.
